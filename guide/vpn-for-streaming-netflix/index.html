Best VPNs for Streaming Netflix in 2026

Netflix has one of the most aggressive VPN detection systems of any streaming platform, and most VPNs fail to consistently bypass it. The ones that work do so through frequently rotated IP addresses and dedicated streaming servers — not all VPNs invest in this infrastructure. This guide ranks the VPNs that reliably unblock Netflix regional libraries based on server coverage, streaming speeds, and consistency of access. Note: using a VPN with Netflix may be against Netflix's terms of service — check the terms for your region before subscribing.

Our Top Picks — 4 tools evaluated

#1

NordVPN

The most consistent Netflix unblocking with the fastest streaming speeds tested.

Best for

Users who want reliable access to 30+ Netflix regional libraries at maximum streaming speeds

Strengths

  • Unblocks 30+ Netflix regional libraries including US, UK, Japan, Canada, and Australia consistently
  • NordLynx protocol delivers ~900 Mbps — no buffering on 4K HDR streams even on long-distance servers
  • SmartDNS feature provides faster streaming on devices that don't support VPN apps (Smart TVs, consoles)

Limitation

Renewal pricing is notably higher than the introductory rate — factor in the long-term cost when choosing your plan length.

Premium streaming performance across 105+ countries — the easiest setup for any device.

Best for

Streamers who want reliable Netflix unblocking on Smart TVs, consoles, and mobile without technical setup

Strengths

Bottom Line

Our Verdict

NordVPN is the top pick for Netflix streaming — 30+ regional libraries, fastest tested speeds, and a SmartDNS option for devices that don't support VPN apps. ExpressVPN is worth the premium if device compatibility matters most (it has the best router and Smart TV support). Surfshark is the right call for households — unlimited devices at the lowest price covers the most common streaming use case without paying per device. Proton VPN's free tier is a useful way to test Netflix unblocking before committing to a paid plan, though its streaming library breadth is more limited. Whichever you choose, use the 30-day money-back guarantee to verify it works with Netflix before the refund window closes.
